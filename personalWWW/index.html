Maze Generator

This is a maze genroator that I wrote in c and displayed using the gfx graphics libraries. The generator creates a random walk which ends once it connects to the already-made path. Once a new random walk is generated and connected to the maze, a new random walk is created from the branching position. The end product consists of one single path from the start to the end with several branching paths which terminate before the end. Click here to view the source code.

Maya Scene

This is a scene that I modeled and animated using the autodesk Maya program. This scene consists of four separate scenes combined through the magic of video editing. The first scene consists of a galaxy created with the software's particles feature. The second scene shows the sun and the earth. The third scene shows an astroid hurtling towards our doomed planet. The asteroids tail was created with the fluids feature of autodesk Maya. The final scene shows the climactic destruction of a lone volcano whose final action was the destruction of its surroundings just as a star dies; destroying any surrounding life. Super Mario Bros.

This is my version of super mario bros. I created one level in c utilizing the gfx graphics libraries. Within the level, the user playing as mario can move, jump, and throw fire balls. The objective is the reach the castle at the end of the level. If the player jumps up into a brown brick, the brick breaks. If the player jumps up into a yellow brick with a question mark, it turns into a brown brick and the player gets points. If the player runs into a goompa???, he loses points. If the player runs into a goompa with 0 points, he loses. Finally, the player can jump on a goompa or hit it with a fire ball which will kill it. Click here to view the source code.
